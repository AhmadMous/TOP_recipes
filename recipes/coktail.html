Spicy Sparkling Cucumber Cocktail

Description:

This is the third best thing ever

This is a spicy yet cool and refreshing cocktail that is perfect for a hot summer day.

    Ingredients:
  • 6 thin slices cucumber
  • 2 ounces juice
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • 3 dashes hot pepper sauce
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 1 cup club soda, or as needed
  • 2 thin slices cucumber
    Steps:
  1. Muddle 6 cucumber slices in a cocktail shaker. Add vodka, lime juice, simple syrup, and hot sauce. Fill shaker with ice and shake for 30 seconds.
  2. Fill 2 glasses with fresh ice. Strain vodka mixture into the glasses and top off with club soda. Garnish with remaining cucumber slices.
